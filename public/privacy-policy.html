Privacy Policy

We believe in keeping things simple and transparent. Here is our privacy policy in plain language.

Email & Subscriptions

We collect your email address when you subscribe to ThingSource. We use it only to send daily ThingSource emails. We never sell your email to anyone, ever. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link at the bottom of any email we send you.

Cookies & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to show advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Third-Party Services

We use a few trusted third-party services to operate ThingSource:

  • Google AdSense (for serving advertisements)
  • Resend (for managing subscribers and sending emails)
  • Netlify (for hosting the website and running serverless functions)

Children's Privacy

This site is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.
