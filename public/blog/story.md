# What is ThingSource?

Welcome to **ThingSource**—your daily dose of curious discoveries and simplified science! 

Every morning at 7:00 AM UTC, an autonomous research system springs to life. Its mission? To seek out the hidden histories behind the everyday objects we take for granted, curate modern humor, and translate the latest cutting-edge scientific breakthroughs into simple stories that anyone can enjoy.

Here is a breakdown of what ThingSource does and the daily digests we deliver to your inbox.

---

## 1. Curious Origins Daily
Every day, the system searches history for surprising origin stories of everyday things—food, words, customs, and inventions. It researches authentic historical origins, verified dates, key figures, and common myths. It then compiles these findings into a warm, engaging daily article written in a direct, concrete style. Whether it's the accidental invention of the Post-it note or the unexpected history of the croissant, you'll learn something new every day.

## 2. Joke of the Day
To start your morning with a smile, the system curates a witty, clean joke or one-liner. It draws inspiration from dry, observational modern stand-up comedy—relatable observations about technology, careers, and modern social life. It delivers a quick punchline to brighten your morning, without any unnecessary filler.

## 3. ScienceSimple: Daily Science Made Easy
Our newest addition, **ScienceSimple**, is dedicated to making science accessible to kids and curious minds alike. The system automatically fetches the top science news of the day from ScienceDaily. For each discovery, it uses advanced AI to rewrite complex research papers and articles into simple, fun, and engaging stories. 
- **Simple Analogies:** Explaining complicated jargon using simple concepts (like comparing the brain's multitasking pathways to highway express lanes).
- **Mind-Blowing Facts:** Curating a list of "Did you know?" facts to keep readers excited about the universe.
- **Scientific Attribution:** Every story points directly to the original scientific paper or study, so readers can verify the source.

---

## How it All Works (The Automation Loop)
ThingSource is fully autonomous, running entirely on serverless scheduled automation:
1. **RSS & Search Grounding:** The system monitors top science and history databases.
2. **AI Translation & Curation:** It uses advanced language models to compile, simplify, and refine the daily issue.
3. **Automatic Publishing:** The issue is instantly written to our database and rendered on our clean web portal.
4. **Email Broadcast:** A daily newsletter containing the full story, joke, and simplified science digest is sent out automatically to all subscribed readers.

By combining automation and education, ThingSource keeps learning fun, accessible, and completely free for everyone.
